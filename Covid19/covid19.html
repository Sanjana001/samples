Coronavirus disease(COVID-19) is an infectious disease which is caused by the SARS-CoV2 virus. This virus belongs to the family of coronavirus which has come from animals.The SARS-Cov2 stands for the "Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus".This virus has the longest incubation period of about 14 days. This virus spreads through the droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes.It has infected around 192 countries of the world. There are so much rumours about the origin of this virus. The first case of this virus was reported in Wuhan,China. China came to know about this virus when they observed that there was a great load of patients suffering from pneumonia in the hospitals of Wuhan.

It's symptoms are:

  • Runny Nose
  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Sore Throat

It's precautions are:

  • Wash your hands for atleast 20 seconds
  • Avoid touching your nose,eyes and mouth
  • If you are suffering from fever,tiredness etc then immediately go to the hospital.
  • Cover your cough or sneeze with tissue and dispose it properly.
